About Latitude Zero Cottages Kikorongo

Latitude Zero Cottages Kikorongo is a peaceful accommodation destination located in Kikorongo, near the breathtaking landscapes of Queen Elizabeth National Park. We offer comfortable cottages, spacious rooms, delicious meals, refreshing drinks, beautiful natural surroundings, reliable 24/7 internet connection, and warm personalized hospitality.

Our goal is to give every guest a comfortable and memorable stay, whether they are visiting for adventure, relaxation, family time, or a peaceful retreat.
